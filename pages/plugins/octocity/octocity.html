About | Under Development

OctoCity is a Grasshopper plugin designed for urban morphology studies and sustainable city design, developed independently by NexoNest. It integrates morphological analysis, clustering algorithms, and parametric modeling to help designers shape resilient and efficient urban environments.

The plugin offers a suite of components for extracting urban form metrics, performing spatial clustering, visualizing morphological patterns, and generating data‑driven city layouts. These tools empower urban planners and architects to make informed decisions in early design phases.

Built with a research‑driven mindset, OctoCity bridges the gap between urban data analysis and design execution, providing a standalone tool that elevates city-scale modeling workflows.

Goals

OctoCity was created to introduce urban morphology analysis and clustering techniques into sustainable city design workflows. Its primary aim is to help practitioners explore form‑based metrics and spatial patterns that influence urban resilience.

Through interactive investigations, users can examine how block density, street network structure, and open‑space distribution affect city performance. This hands‑on approach deepens understanding of urban form and supports evidence‑based planning.

Evolving beyond research, OctoCity is now being fine‑tuned for professional use, offering robust components that aid in real‑world urban design challenges, from neighborhood revitalization to masterplanning of new districts.
