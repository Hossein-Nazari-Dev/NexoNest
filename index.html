What We Do

At NexoNext, we are revolutionizing the future of sustainable design through cutting-edge digital tools. Our expertise lies in BIM (Building Information Modeling), BEM (Building Energy Modeling), and UBEM (Urban Building Energy Modeling), enabling architects, engineers, and urban planners to create smarter, more efficient, and environmentally friendly spaces.

We specialize in Agent-Based Modeling, which allows us to simulate human behavior, energy consumption, and comfort levels within built environments. By leveraging advanced simulations, we help you optimize designs for energy efficiency, thermal comfort, and overall sustainability.

Our mission is to empower professionals to design a better future—one where technology and nature coexist harmoniously. Whether you're designing a single building or an entire city, our tools provide the insights you need to make informed, sustainable decisions.

Join us in shaping a sustainable future. Together, we can create spaces that are not only functional and beautiful but also kind to our planet.
